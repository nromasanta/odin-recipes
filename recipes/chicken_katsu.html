Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu is a Japanese dish of fried chicken made with panko bread crumbs.

Generally served with tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts pounded to 1/2 thickness
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup of oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Season chicken breast on both sides with salt and pepper
  2. Place flour, egg, and panko into separate shallow dishes
  3. Coat chicken breasts in flour, shaking off excess
  4. Dip into egg and press into panko crumbs until well coated
  5. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet
  6. Place chicken in oil and cook 3-4 minutes per side or until golden brown
