Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu is a Japanese dish of fried chicken made with panko bread crumbs.

Generally served with tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts pounded to 1/2 thickness
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup of oil for frying, or as needed
